Historical Origin

The text comes from sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 of "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (The Extremes of Good and Evil) by Cicero, written in 45 BC. It is a treatise on ethical theory.

Why not just use "Text"?

Repeatedly copying "Text here text here" creates a distracting pattern. Lorem Ipsum has a realistic distribution of letters and word lengths, simulating normal English (or any Latin-based language) flow.

Latin Authenticity
