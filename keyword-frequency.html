What is Keyword Density?

Keyword density is the percentage of times a keyword or phrase appears compared to the total number of words on the page.

  • The Goldilocks Zone

    Too little (< 0.5%)> Google might not realize the page is about that topic. Too much (> 3%) > You might be penalized for "keyword stuffing".

  • Semantic SEO

    Modern SEO focuses less on repeating the same word and more on using related concepts (LSI keywords).
