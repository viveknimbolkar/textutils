Technical Details

Case Styles

**CamelCase** joins words without spaces and capitalizes each new word. **Snake_case** joins words with underscores. **Kebab-case** uses hyphens and is standard for URLs.

Transformation Logic

The converter first "tokenizes" the input string (splits it into words based on spaces or existing delimiters) and then reconstructs it applying the specific capitalization rules of the target format.

Locale Awareness

Our tool respects locale-specific rules where possible. For instance, in Turkish, the capital of `i` is `İ`, which differs from the standard English conversion.
