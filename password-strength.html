Why Password Entropy Matters

Length is often more important than complexity. A 20-character phrase is harder to crack than an 8-character complex string.

  • Brute Force Protection

    Every extra bit of entropy doubles the time required to guess the key.

  • Dictionary Attacks

    Using random words avoids common dictionary hacks used by attackers.
