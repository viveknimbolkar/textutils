Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to TextUtils. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website.

2. Data Collection (Client-Side Only)

Crucially, our tools are designed to run Client-Side. This means when you paste text into our Word Counter, Case Converter, or JSON Formatter, that text is processed entirely within your web browser's memory. It is NOT sent to our servers. We do not see, store, or process your input text.

3. Analytics

We may use basic analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to track page visits and tool usage counts. This data is anonymized and does not contain any of the text content you process.

4. Cookies

We use local storage to save your preferences (such as Dark Mode). We may use third-party cookies for advertising (Google AdSense) to serve relevant ads.
