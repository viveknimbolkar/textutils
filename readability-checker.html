Why Readability Matters

In the digital age, attention spans are short. Complex writing drives visitors away.

  • SEO Impact

    Search engines prefer content that provides quick, clear answers. High bounce rates from confusing text hurt rankings.

  • User Experience

    Accessible content builds trust. If users can't understand your product description, they won't buy.

Improvement Tips

1

2
