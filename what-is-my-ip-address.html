Frequently Asked Questions About IP Addresses

What is my IP address used for?

Your IP address allows websites to send data to your device and can reveal your approximate location.

Can someone find me with my IP?

Your IP only reveals your city or region, not your exact address or identity.

Why does my IP address change?

ISPs assign dynamic IPs that change periodically. VPNs or router restarts can also change it.

How do I hide my IP address?

Use a VPN or proxy service to mask your real IP and protect your privacy online.
