Importance of Structure

Headings are the skeleton of your content. Google uses them to understand what your page is widely about and what the sub-sections cover.

  • Accessibility

    Screen readers allow users to jump from heading to heading. Broken hierarchy makes this navigation confusing.

  • Featured Snippets

    Well-structured H2s and H3s are often pulled by Google for "People Also Ask" answers.
